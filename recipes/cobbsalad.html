Cobb Salad

Description

The Cobb salad is an American garden salad. This salad contains tomatoes, eggs, chicken, blue cheese and avocado.

Ingredients

  • 6 slices bacon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 head iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 3 cups chopped, cooked chicken meat
  • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
  • Â¾ cup blue cheese, crumbled
  • 3 green onions, chopped
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted and diced
  • 1 (8 ounce) bottle Ranch-style salad dressing

Steps

  1. Place eggs in a saucepan and cover completely with cold water; bring to a boil, then cover and remove from heat.
  2. Let eggs sit for 10 to 12 minutes, then cool, peel and chop.
  3. While the eggs are cooking, place bacon in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly brown, 7 to 10 minutes.
  4. Drain, crumble, and set aside.
  5. Divide shredded lettuce among individual plates. Top with rows of bacon, eggs, chicken, tomatoes, blue cheese, green onions, and avocado.
  6. Drizzle with dressing.
