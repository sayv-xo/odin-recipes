Cantaloupe Frenzy

Description

Cantaloupe is light refreshing smoothie, perfect for hot summer days or after school snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cantaloupe - peeled, seeded and cubed
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar, or to taste
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place cantaloupe cubes and ice into the container of a blender.
  2. Process until the ice is in small pieces.
  3. Add sugar, and puree.
  4. Pour into tall glasses and serve immediately.
